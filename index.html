Onigiri

  • Time: 15min~1hr
  • Serves: A lot
Ingredients Quantity
  • Short grain rice
  • Salt
  • Seaweed
  • Filling (of any kind)
  • 1-4 cups
  • A pinch
  • 1-2 sheets for each onigiri
  • However much you'd like

Instructions

Step 1: Cook your rice.
Step 2: When rice is cooked, mix it around with your paddle in your
pot or rice cooker.
Step 3: Start by lightly salting your hands, or saran wrap to place rice in.
Step 4: If you would like to put some fillings in your onigiri,
place them in the middle of the pile of rice you created.
(Here are some examples of fillings you can use.)
You will then surround it with rice, and mold the rice into the
triangular shape. You can use a mold or your hands to make the triangular shape.
Step 5: When you mold the rice into the shape you desire,
cover a side of the onigiri with a sheet of Nori seaweed (as shown in picture above).
Step 6: You are done. Enjoy!

Isa's Signature Bagel Sandwich

  • Time: 15~30min
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
  • Bagel of your choice
  • Egg
  • Ham and/or Bacon
  • Cheese (cheddar or white)
  • Mayonaise
  • However many you need (1 per person usually)
  • 1 per sandwich
  • As much as you'd like
  • As much as you'd like
  • As much as you'd like

Instructions

Step 1: Prep your ingredients! Slice your bagel in half, as shown below.

Step 1 Con't: Prep your eggs how you'd like to cook them
(I personally like sunny side ups or scrambled eggs)
→ Various egg recipes ←
Step 2: Toast your bagel for approximately 2 minutes.
Step 3: While your bagel is toasting,
pre heat and oil your pan to cook your eggs.
Don't forget to add salt and pepper to your eggs!

Tip! consider melting your cheese while your eggs are cooking!

Step 4: You can use store bought ham, but with bacon, consider
cooking them on the same pan with the eggs and cheese.
Step 5: When your bagel is done toasting, and your eggs are cooked,
assemble your bagel sandwich in this order:

  1. Mayonaise on bottom half of bagel
  2. Place your eggs and cheese on top
  3. Ham and/or Bacon
  4. Top half of bagel

Step 6: You may cut your bagel in half to share, or for convenience.
Step 7: You are done. Enjoy!
